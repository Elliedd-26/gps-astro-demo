# Trails We Are Not Translating

Eight of us met at Rouge Park at 9am on a Saturday. Some had hiking boots. Some had Converse. One person brought a thermos of bubble tea, which became the most envied object of the morning.

We did not plan an agenda. That was the point.

![A group of women walking on a forest trail](/images/hiking/02.jpg)
*Walking single file because the trail is narrow. Talking in three languages because that is how we talk.*

## What we did

- Walked about 6 km on the Mast Trail
- Stopped whenever anyone wanted to stop  
- Ate lunch on a fallen log overlooking a creek
- Traded snacks (pineapple buns, La Yu chips, granola bars)
- Did not talk about work

The not-talking-about-work was an accidental rule. Someone mentioned a deadline in the first ten minutes, and another person gently said "we are not doing that here," and that was that.

![Hikers sitting on logs eating lunch](/images/hiking/03.jpg)
*Lunch on a fallen log. Someone brought way too many pineapple buns. There were no leftovers.*

## What is next

We are doing this again in July. Different trail, same vibe. The slowest pace sets the pace.

The trees are not going anywhere.

# 我们不必翻译的山路

我们八个人，周六早上九点在Rouge公园集合。有人穿了正经的登山鞋，有人穿了Converse。有人带了一保温杯珍珠奶茶——成为整个早上最被羡慕的物品。

我们没有计划任何议程。这就是重点。

![一群女性走在森林小径上](/images/hiking/02.jpg)
*因为小路窄，我们排成单列走。因为我们就是这么说话的——用三种语言。*

## 我们做了什么

- 走了大约6公里的Mast Trail
- 任何人想停下来就停下来
- 在一根倒下的树干上吃午饭
- 交换零食（菠萝包、老干妈味薯片、燕麦棒）
- 不聊工作

不聊工作是一条意外的规则。有人在最初十分钟提到一个deadline，另一个人很温柔地说"我们在这里不做那个"，然后就是这样。

![徒步者坐在树干上吃饭](/images/hiking/03.jpg)
*在倒下的树干上吃午饭。有人带了太多菠萝包。最后一个都没剩下。*

## 接下来

我们七月还会再办一次。不同的小路，一样的氛围。最慢的人决定速度。

树不会去哪里。
